Privacy Policy

Last updated: 29 June 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Word Search (the “App”), published by Nofil Bashir (“we”, “us”, or the “Developer”), handles information. By using the App, you agree to the practices described here.

We do not require you to create an account, and we do not knowingly collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, or precise location from within our Apps. Our Apps are casual offline games. Some limited information is collected automatically by the third-party services described below, primarily to display ads and understand basic usage.

Information collected automatically

When you use an App, the third-party services we integrate may collect:

  • A device advertising identifier (e.g. Google Advertising ID).
  • Approximate (non-precise) location derived from your IP address.
  • Device information such as model, operating-system version, and language.
  • App usage and diagnostic events (for example, levels started or completed, and crash data).

This information is processed by the third parties on their own infrastructure. We access it only in aggregated, non-identifying form.

Third-party services we use

  • Google AdMob — serves the advertisements shown in our Apps and may use the advertising identifier to deliver and measure ads. See How Google uses information from sites or apps that use its services.
  • Google Analytics for Firebase — provides anonymous, aggregated usage and stability statistics so we can improve our Apps. See the Firebase Privacy and Security information.

These services are operated by Google LLC and are governed by the Google Privacy Policy.

How information is used

  • To display and measure advertising (including, where permitted, personalized ads).
  • To understand which features are used and to fix crashes and bugs.
  • To keep our Apps secure and working reliably.

We do not sell your personal information.

Your choices

  • You can reset or delete your advertising identifier, or opt out of ad personalization, in your device settings (Android: Settings → Google → Ads).
  • Where required by law, we request your consent for personalized advertising the first time you open an App, and you can change that choice from within the App.

Children’s privacy

Our Apps are intended for a general audience and are not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it.

Data retention

We do not store personal data on our own servers. Data collected by the third-party services above is retained according to their respective policies.
